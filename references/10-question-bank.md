# Question Bank

Use clean, single-focus questions. Do not ask too many at once.

## Contract

- "What would make this conversation valuable for you?"
- "What do you want to have by the end of our time?"
- "What is the best controllable result we can work toward now?"
- "What first step would be most useful to clarify today?"

## From Complaint To Desire

- "If you do not want this, what do you want?"
- "What is the positive direction behind this frustration?"
- "What would you like to create instead?"
- "Within your control, what can be different?"

## Values

- "Why does this matter to you?"
- "What value is asking for attention here?"
- "What would this give you that is even more important?"
- "What part of you comes alive when you imagine this?"

## Outcome

- "What do you truly want?"
- "What would the best result look like?"
- "What would you see, hear, and feel if this were already happening?"
- "What is the outcome above the outcome?"

## Path

- "How can you get it?"
- "What are three possible ways?"
- "What resources would help?"
- "What system would make this easier?"
- "What is the smallest testable step?"

## Control

- "What is within your control here?"
- "What can you influence, prepare for, request, or choose?"
- "What feels uncontrollable, and how might part of it become workable?"
- "What would raise your sense of control by one point?"

## Commitment

- "How committed are you to this?"
- "What would deepen your commitment?"
- "What obstacle might appear?"
- "What is your B plan?"
- "What decision do you need to make?"

## Evidence

- "How will you know you have got it?"
- "What signal will prove progress?"
- "What will be visible when this is complete?"
- "How will you know you are on track?"

## Logical Levels

- "Who do you want to become here?"
- "What value does this express?"
- "What belief would support this?"
- "What capability do you need?"
- "What action fits that identity?"
- "When and where will it happen?"
- "Who else benefits?"

## Action

- "What will you do this week?"
- "What are three small actions?"
- "When exactly will you begin?"
- "Where will you do it?"
- "Will you make this commitment?"

## Closing

- "What value did this conversation create?"
- "What is clearer now?"
- "What commitment are you taking with you?"
- "How will you support yourself after this conversation?"
