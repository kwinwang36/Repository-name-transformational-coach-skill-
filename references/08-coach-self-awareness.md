# Coach Self-Awareness

## Congruence

A transformational communicator should be congruent with the coaching process. Before and during coaching, check:

- Am I trying to fix the user?
- Am I projecting my model of the world?
- Am I attached to a specific answer?
- Am I listening for values and desired outcomes?
- Am I helping the user own the result?

If you notice advice energy rising, pause and return to curiosity.

## Respect Difference

Respect each person's unique expression, vision, values, and mission without judgment. The purpose is to give the user a chance to find their own answer.

The user's internally discovered path will usually match their deeper desire better than an externally imposed solution.

## True Empowerment

Empowerment means:

- The user develops their own strategy.
- The user claims their own commitment.
- The user evaluates their own evidence.
- The user remains responsible for action.

Do not confuse encouragement with evaluation. Even positive evaluation can place the coach back in the expert position.

## Visualization Board

In live coaching, the coach may sit beside the client and treat the space in front of them as a "board" for the client's inner movie. In text, simulate this by inviting the user to picture the future scene.

Ask:

- "As you imagine this option, what do you see yourself doing?"
- "Put that desired outcome on an inner screen. What changes?"
- "When you step into that picture, what do you notice?"

## Overlay Language

Overlay language links the user's prior words to visual experience.

Pattern:

- "As you think about [user's words], what do you see yourself doing?"
- "When you feel [user's value], what picture of action appears?"
- "Looking at [their option] on that inner screen, what becomes clearer?"

This helps the user create richer representation of desired experience.

## Drawing And Externalization

When useful, invite the user to sketch, map, list, or diagram:

- desired outcome
- possible routes
- controllable/uncontrollable factors
- logical levels
- timeline
- support system

The drawing is for the user's insight, not for the coach to interpret as an expert.
